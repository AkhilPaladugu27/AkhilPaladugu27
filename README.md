### 🧠 About Me

I'm Akhil — an **AI-powered full-stack engineer** and the founder behind **[SkillVolume](https://skillvolume.com)**, where I design and ship intelligent web products and growth systems end-to-end: architecture, frontend, backend, and the AI layer that ties it together.

My engineering focus sits at three intersections:

- **Full-stack product engineering** — modern, performant web apps built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, taken from architecture to production.
- **Applied AI/LLM engineering** — practical LLM integrations (OpenAI API) for content generation, automation, and growth tooling rather than research-only prototypes.
- **Product engineering mindset** — I ship as a founder, which means every feature is scoped against real user/business outcomes, not just technical correctness.

**Open To:**
- 🤝 Freelance / contract full-stack or AI-tooling engagements
- 🧩 Collaborating on open-source AI, web-dev, or growth-tooling projects
- 💼 Full-time Software / AI Engineering roles where product ownership matters
